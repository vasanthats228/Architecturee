OUR STORY

From Architects to Software Creators

Founded in 2018 by three practicing architects frustrated with clunky BIM software, Stackly began as a mission to create tools that actually understand how architects work.

Sarah, Michael, and Elena met at Foster + Partners, where they spent years wishing for better software. Today, their vision serves over 12 million architects worldwide.
